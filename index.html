Today’s Challenge

The Pressure Is Real—But So Are the Solutions

Culinary teams are navigating rising costs, plastic waste, and growing sustainability demands—all while trying to keep operations running smoothly. Topanga’s tools help culinary teams overcome these challenges for real impact.

Problem

What culinary teams are up against

  • Rising food costs are unpredictable and hard to control
  • Single-use plastics are everywhere—and avoidable
  • Consumers want sustainability, but not at the cost of convenience

Solution

What smarter culinary teams are doing instead

  • Cutting costs by acting on StreamLine’s robust, accurate production data
  • Swapping single-use for a seamless and trackable ReusePass reusables program
  • Hitting sustainability goals without sacrificing convenience and ease
